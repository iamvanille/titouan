Bio

Based in the heart of the French Riviera, I strive to create striking imagery of the dream-like spaces that dot this iconic part of our world. Seeing sights through the eyes of a landscape photographer, I like to photograph the built environment - from the charming Bastides of Provence, to the lavish villas of the Coast and the yachts that sail it - as continuous to the natural, a man-made landscape if you will.

Aside from Hospitality spaces and Landscapes, Ongoing Works include photographing the historic tramlines of Europe, most notably in countries of the former Soviet Union.

All prints are available for sale in limited editions of 30. To order, or to book a shoot anywhere in the world, please get in touch using the contact details.
