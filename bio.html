About

Born in the picturesque French Riviera, photography was not my first hobby. At around 6, I began playing drums, an instrument which has fascinated me since. During my high-school years, I started diving deeper into photography, something which I had always been drawn to but never taken too seriously. I mostly put drumming on hold when I left home to go study in Montreal, and photography took over. Cameras tend to be far more portable than drumsets.

I'm especially drawn to clean, open and simple but stunning landscapes - places like Tuscany, the Dolomites and the lavender fields of Valensole, just a couple of hours driving from home.
